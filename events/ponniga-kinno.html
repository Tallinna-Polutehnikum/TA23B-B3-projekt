Baby-friendly Cinema

Every month on the second Wednesday at 10:30

We welcome cinema lovers big and small to Apollo Kino Ülemiste for baby-friendly screenings! These sessions are created so you can enjoy a good film with your little companion — comfortably, safely, and with consideration for young children.

Why choose Baby-friendly Cinema?

  • Softer sound and a dimmed auditorium
  • Two films screened — one for adults and one for children
  • Comfortable and safe environment
  • Adapted to the needs of young children

Practical info:

  • Occurs every month on the second Wednesday
  • Starts at 10:30
  • Limited seating available
