Andre Rieu 2025 Christmas Concert

December 2025

This holiday season, magic comes to the big screen. Join André Rieu for his 2025 Christmas concert "Merry Christmas" and enjoy beloved carols, beautiful waltzes and plenty of surprises — the ultimate holiday cinema event!

Together with his magnificent Johann Strauss Orchestra and special guests, including the sensational Emma Kok and over 400 wind players delivering a majestic Christmas sound, André's concert is full of warmth, laughter and festive joy. Christmas is André's favorite time of year — and he can't wait to share this grand concert with you, exclusively in cinemas!

Concert details:

  • Full-length concert on the big screen
  • Johann Strauss Orchestra
  • Special guest: Emma Kok
  • Over 400 wind players
  • Exclusive Christmas repertoire
  • Only in cinemas
